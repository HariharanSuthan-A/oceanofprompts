Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our prompt packs

What exactly do I get when I purchase a prompt pack?

Each prompt pack contains a collection of specialized AI prompts designed for a specific website type. You'll receive a document with carefully crafted prompts that you can copy and paste into your no-code AI builder to generate website sections, layouts, and content.

Do I need coding experience to use these prompts?

Absolutely not! These prompts are designed specifically for no-code platforms. All you need to do is copy the prompt text and paste it into your AI website builder. The AI will handle the rest.

Can I use these prompts with any AI website builder?

Our prompts work with most popular AI-powered website builders including Durable, Lovable, CreateDesign, Framer AI, and Mixo. The prompts are designed to be platform-agnostic and should work with any builder that accepts text prompts.

How often do you update the prompt packs?

We regularly update our prompt packs based on user feedback and improvements in AI technology. When you purchase a pack, you'll receive free updates for at least one year.

Can I use these prompts for client projects?

Yes! Our prompt packs include a commercial license that allows you to use the generated websites for your clients. You can create unlimited websites for yourself or your clients.
