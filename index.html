Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI prompts

How often do you update your AI prompts? Updated Daily

We update our AI prompt library every single day! Our team of prompt engineers works continuously to create new prompts and refine existing ones based on the latest AI developments and user feedback. Each day, we add at least 5-10 new prompts across various categories to ensure you always have fresh, effective tools for your projects.

Can I use these prompts for commercial projects?

Absolutely! All our prompts come with a commercial license that allows you to use them in your personal, professional, and client projects. There are no restrictions on how many projects you can create using our prompts. We designed them specifically to help creators like you build amazing products.

How do I access prompt updates after purchasing?

When you purchase any prompt pack, you get lifetime access to all future updates for that pack. We'll notify you via email whenever new prompts are added to packs you've purchased. You can also log in to your account on our website to download the latest versions at any time.

Do I need coding experience to use these prompts?

Not at all! Our prompts are designed specifically for no-code platforms. All you need to do is copy the prompt text and paste it into your AI website builder of choice. The prompts guide the AI to generate exactly what you need without any technical knowledge required.

How can I request specific prompt types?

We love receiving prompt requests from our community! You can submit your prompt ideas through Email. where we regularly take suggestions. Many of our daily updates are inspired by user requests, so don't hesitate to share what you'd like to see.
